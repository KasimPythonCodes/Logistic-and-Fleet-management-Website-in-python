What is Vgo App?

Vgo provides affordable and reliable moving services. Just log in, choose a mini-truck or 2-wheeler, and book! Get a verified driver with an ETA of under 15 minutes. How to use the Vgo app?

Download the Vgo App,

Choose where to pick up and drop off. Choose from 2 wheelers and mini trucks (Tata Ace, Pickup 8ft, Pickup 7ft, Tempo, etc). Vehicle arrives in under 15 minutes.

What is the process for booking a tempo/mini truck or bike online with Vgo?

Below are the steps to book a vehicle: Sign into the Vgo app

Browse through the available vehicles (Tata Ace, 8ft pickup truck, 2 wheeler, etc) and select the vehicle you want. You'll need to enter a few more details, apply a coupon for a discount, and then confirm your booking. Sit back and relax, our reliable driver partners and technology will handle the rest.

What are charges for a Vgo truck and Vgo bike delivery services?

The cost of renting a Vgo truck or Vgo bike is based on the number of miles they travel, type of vehicle used and varies by location. You can also use the estimate option on the Vgo website to receive a more precise estimate of the cost of your journey.
